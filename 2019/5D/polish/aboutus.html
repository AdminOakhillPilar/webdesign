Who we are

Polish Buffalo Roller is an innovative clothing brand that was created in order to give the customer what they were looking for; It is a brand that encompasses all types of style and sizes. That way it makes the customer feel happy with clothes and fashion, without any discrimination.The brand was created on January 24, 1996, by entrepreneurs Anselmo Yanzon and Manuel Forconi. It has no special products, but covers everything. From sports to formal, from slippers to shirts, as well as accessories and perfumes. In 1995 the creators bought the Mauritz Widforss hunting weapons store in Stockholm to dispose of the commercial premises, with the aim of buying and selling clothes. But thanks to the economic crisis of 1994, the project could not be launched until the crisis passed in mid-1995.

Anselmo Yanzón

Anselmo Yanzón was born the 10 of June of 1961. He was the son of a deli merchant in Västerås (Sweden) and decided from the beginning to become an entrepreneur. He devoted himself to selling most of the time. His first great success came in 1996, when with his colleague Manuel Forconi invent the famous clothing business chain, called "Polish Buffalo Roller." He married his fifth, Argentine and, for the moment, wife Anna Watson, owner of the famous American brand "Husstty." Anselmo Yanzon lives on the outskirts of Sweden with his wife and four children, Sergio, Lázaro, Anna J. and Lucia.

Manuel Forconi

Manuel Forconi was born in Poznan, Poland, on September 26, 1959. He had a life full of luxuries conceived by his upper-class family. He enrolled him in one of the best schools in Poland, where by his high academic level he managed to reach the prestigious university of Oxford. There he met a girl, also from Poland, who later became his wife and partner of the company. Nowadays Manuel dedicates his life to the successful company that he achieved with his associate.
