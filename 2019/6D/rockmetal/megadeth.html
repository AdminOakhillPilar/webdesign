Megadeth is an American thrash metal band, formed in Los Angeles, California. It was created in 1983 by Dave Mustaine (vocalist, guitarist and lead composer), after he was expelled from Metallica, where he held the position of lead guitarist. Megadeth is known for his distinctive style on guitars, Mustaine's peculiar way of singing, the musical complexity conformed by the great technique that his music contains, and the themes of his lyrics, often on the anti-system, politics, war, and , on several occasions, sentimental relationships. Today it is made up of Dave Mustaine (rhythmic guitar / vocals), Kiko Loureiro (lead guitar), David Ellefson (bass) and Dirk Verbeuren (drums)
