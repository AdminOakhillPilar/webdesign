Metallica is, according to a lot of people, the single greatest band of all time. Metallica was founded by drummer Lars Ulrich and rythmic guitarist/vocals James Hetfield in 1981 in Los Angeles and then traveled to San Francisco. Later on, the band (formed by James Hetfield, Lars Ulrich, Kirk Hammett and Cliff Burton) released their first album, Kill Em All (1983). Nowadays Metallica posses 10 studio albums

  • Kill Em All 1983
  • Ride The Lightning 1984
  • Master Of Puppets 1986
  • ...And Justice For All 1988
  • Metallica 1991
  • Load 1996
  • Reload 1997
  • St Anger 2003
  • Death Magnetic 2008
  • Hardwired... To Self-Destruct 2016
