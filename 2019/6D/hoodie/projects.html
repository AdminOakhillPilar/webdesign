As you must know the situation, the ocean has become a bit complicated, the situation is getting out of hand, having as a consequence the loss of many species and generating great pollution in the world. Every second it is estimated that 200 kilos of plastic are thrown into the ocean, that is, it is estimated that approximately 8 million tons are thrown into the ocean every year! Given this situation we decided to take the initiative and face the problem once and for all. With Hoodie-OG we collect the plastic of the ocean and based on it we design our high quality products, offering them to the public in order to reinvest 100 percent of their income in the aforementioned cause. With this we seek to improve the situation of the planet to care for the future of our children and preserving many species that are being lost today. And in turn keeping our products in the highest quality and fashion wing.
