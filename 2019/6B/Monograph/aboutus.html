Who are we?

All of this, include different topics such as, love, marriage, social relationships, girl power, etc. and what we did was analyze all of this during the roaring twenties under thye light of this awesome book Fitzgerald wrote.


With the help of Giorgina Fassano Aurio, literature teacher and specialist on English acadremic language, we made different papers, all of them written with different arguments and points of view. We also supported some of our arguments with two philosophical texts; La Confusion del Yo by Giussani and Emma Palesse's article about Baumann's liquid modernity deconstruction. Both of them, helped us to understand the text and to create and write stronger arguments about society and their objectives on the Roaring Twenties.
